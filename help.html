Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I log into my account?

Ensure you are using the correct email and password. If the problem persists, try resetting your password.

How do I place an order?

Select your favorite dishes, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can choose your preferred payment method during checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery and also UPI Payments.

How to buy multiple food items at a time?

You can add the selected food item to cart each time. then go to cart page and click on buy.

What should I do if my order is late?

If your order is delayed, please contact our support team immediately using the provided phone numbers or WhatsApp.
